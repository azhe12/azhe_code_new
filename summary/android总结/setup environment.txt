6..How to download ROM images

 

Fastboot flash image command

$fastboot flash partition file_name

Flash zip file:            

$fastboot flash zip CL.zip

Flash kernel image:

$fastboot flash boot android.vmlinux

Flash hboot image: 

$ fastboot flash hboot android.swbootloader

Flash system image:       

$ fastboot flash system system.img

Flash userdata image:    

$ fastboot flash userdata userdata.img

Flash rootfs image:

$ fastboot flash rootfs rootfs.img
